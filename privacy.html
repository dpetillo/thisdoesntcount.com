Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

This Doesn't Count ("the app") is developed by CodePraxis. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it's used, and your rights.

Data We Collect

None. The app does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

  • All habit data, cheat entries, and settings are stored locally on your device only.
  • No analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind.
  • No account creation or sign-in required.
  • No advertising or ad-related identifiers.

Data Storage

Your data is stored in a local database on your device. If you use the backup/export feature, the exported file is saved to a location you choose on your device. The app never uploads data to any server.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, they are scheduled locally on your device. No notification data is sent to external servers.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since no personal data is collected from any user, no special provisions are necessary.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
